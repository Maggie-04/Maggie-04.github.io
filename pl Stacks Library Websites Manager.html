Stacks for Public Libraries

Stacks empowers librarians to design and create a world-class library website experience without relying on web developers or IT support to curate the right content, resources, and services.

・Drive Awareness and Utilization
Stacks helps you market your library by highlighting programs, resources, content, and events your users care about.

・Integrations Made Easy
Connect all your content and technology with pre-built integrations to common systems and content providers.

・Ease of Administration
No web expertise required! Stacks provides easy-to-use drag-and-drop layouts and tools designed by librarians for librarians.

・Consolidate Events and Bookings Tools
Save money and reduce administrative work by managing events and bookings right within the Stacks Dashboard.
