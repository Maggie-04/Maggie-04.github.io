Pioneer in the library services industry
EBSCO Industries, Inc. was founded in 1944 and acquired EBSCO Publishing in 1987, now EBSCO Information Services (EIS). Starting out as a small subscription agency, EBSCO Information Services, a wholly owned subsidiary of EBSCO Industries, Inc.quickly became a leader in the library services industry.


Reliable information for all kinds of research
EBSCO is a provider for K-12 schools, university, colleges, corporations, public libraries, academic libraries and government worldwide to provide research databases, ebooks, e-journals and e-package subscription management, book collection development and acquisition management.
