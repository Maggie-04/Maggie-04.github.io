EBSCO Discovery Service (EDS)

What can EBSCO Discovery Service do for school community
・Collaborate on class projects
・Build curricula (for all subjects)
・Learn more about hobbies and sports
・Find resources on designing video games
・Discover historical facts and the latest events
・Research colleges and companies.

With EBSCO Discovery Service, students can enter keywords in a single search and see results from several publishers, including:
・Professional development collections(專長發展資源)
・Encyclopedia collections(百科全書全集)
・Biography collections(傳記集)
・Science Reference Center(科學參考中心)
・History Reference Center(歷史參考中心)
・E-books and audiobooks (fiction and nonfiction)(電子書和有聲讀物[小說和非小說類])
・Popular magazines and articles(熱門雜誌和文章)

Features and functionality of EDS:
🔼 Sophisticated Search(頂尖的檢索) ⇨ Using various search options and superior relevance ranking technology.
🔼 Direct Access to Full Text(直接存取全文) ⇨ One-click access directly through the results list.
🔼 Intuitive Interface(直觀的介面) ⇨ Features and technology built on user research.
🔼 Superior Integrations(卓越的整合功能) ⇨ Apps and integrations with multiple platforms.
🔼 Exceptional Support(出色的技術支援) ⇨ Set up and support from our highly skilled staff.
