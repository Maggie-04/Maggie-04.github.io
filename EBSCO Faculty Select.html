EBSCO Faculty Select

EBSCO Faculty Select empowers academic libraries to directly support textbook affordability efforts. Through a single interface, faculty can search and access quality open textbooks, Open Educational Resources (OER), EBSCO’s eBook Open Access (OA) Collection and request access to unrestricted library e-books from top academic publishers.

・Provide open and affordable textbooks
Achieve library goals and meet institutional or state-level mandates surrounding textbook affordability.

・Streamline access to quality OER
Save time searching and collating materials from numerous Open Educational Resources (OER) platforms and the open Web.

・Improve access to course content
Simplify e-book access for students with no concurrent user or content restrictions (printing, saving or downloading).

・Enable faculty to search and request e-books
Provide faculty with access to EBSCO’s eBook OA Collection and high-quality, library-licensed e-books to support course material.
