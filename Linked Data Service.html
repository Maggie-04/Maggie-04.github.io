BiblioGraph for Academics

Users can explore, use, and access your catalog from anywhere on the web. By leveraging BIBFRAME and Linked Data, BiblioGraph connects library resources within a graph, enriches resources with data from authoritative sources, and enables the publishing of library resources on any website.

・Living lists
Curate lists of items such as eBooks, print books, audio materials and other formats. Lists can be by and about authors, on any topic, time, place and more. Lists bring people directly into the catalog and are automatically updated as your catalog evolve.

・Publish and share
Publish your catalog on the open web. Anyone can use your resources from websites or applications, and all links lead back to the library. Embed resources on websites, create custom lists to share for courses, reading groups and more. Also share your resources via email or social media.

・Network connections
With BiblioGraph, your library joins a network that connects your enriched resources to thousands of other graphs. See what your library holds and what is missing from your catalog.

・Embedded data
Embed and share living lists on any website including the Learning Management System, IR, department website, LibGuides, and many more. Embedded data describes what the library offers, creating new pathways into your collections.
